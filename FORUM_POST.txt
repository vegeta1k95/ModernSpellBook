[b][size=18]ModernSpellBook - Retail-inspired Spellbook for Turtle WoW[/size][/b]

Tired of the vanilla spellbook? This addon replaces it with a modern, two-page layout inspired by retail WoW - while keeping the classic parchment feel.

[b]Download:[/b] [url=https://github.com/vegeta1k95/ModernSpellBook]GitHub[/url]

[b][size=14]Features[/size][/b]

[list]
[*][b]Two-page book layout[/b] - beautiful parchment pages with a bookmark divider
[*][b]Tabbed navigation[/b] - Class, General, Pet, Companions, Mounts, Toys - all in one place
[*][b]Professions tab[/b] - automatically separated from General spells
[*][b]Search bar[/b] - quickly find any spell by name or keyword
[*][b]Round passive icons[/b] - passives are visually distinct with round portraits and a blue ring
[*][b]Unlearned spells[/b] - see what spells you haven't learned yet (greyed out with level requirements)
[*][b]Talent-granted spells[/b] - shows which spells come from talents vs trainers
[*][b]"Train" badge[/b] - green badge highlights spells you can learn right now at your level
[*][b]"New" badge[/b] - yellow badge marks freshly learned spells
[*][b]Cooldowns[/b] - cooldown sweep displayed on spell icons
[*][b]Active aura tracking[/b] - active stances/auras glow in real-time
[*][b]Spell tooltips[/b] - full descriptions even for unlearned spells (captured from trainer)
[*][b]Settings menu[/b] - font size, text color (light/dark), highlight toggles, icon frame options
[/list]

[b][size=14]How Unlearned Spells Work[/size][/b]

The spellbook can show spells you haven't learned yet - greyed out with their level requirement. To populate this list:

[list=1]
[*]Visit a class trainer in a major city (starting zone trainers have limited spells)
[*]The addon automatically captures all available spells on your first visit
[*]Open your spellbook - unlearned spells appear at the end of each spec section
[*]A green [b]"Train"[/b] badge appears on spells you can learn at your current level
[*]Hovering over a badge dismisses it
[/list]

Talent-granted spells are also detected automatically - no trainer visit needed for those.

[b][size=14]Settings[/size][/b]

Click the gear icon next to the search bar to access:

[list]
[*][b]Show unlearned[/b] - toggle unlearned spell visibility
[*][b]Highlights[/b] - control glow and badge for learned/available spells independently
[*][b]Font size[/b] - choose from 9 to 13
[*][b]Spell text color[/b] - Light (gold text with shadows) or Dark (black text)
[*][b]Spell icon frame[/b] - toggle fancy borders per spell category
[/list]
